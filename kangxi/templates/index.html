This website presents practical tools for:

  • studying Chinese characters
  • searching characters by components of their internal structure
  • searching characters by their English and Russian meanings
  • analysis of character sets grouped by their common roots or common meanings

Character trees make it possible to order all characters into interrelated sequences solely on the basis of their internal structure.

The About page presents the results of ordering characters on the basis of their internal structure, which led to the development of the search and analysis Tools.
